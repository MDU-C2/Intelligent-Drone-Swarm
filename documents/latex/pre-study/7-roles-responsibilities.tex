\section{Roles \& Responsibilities}
The IRDS project is organised according to a role-based structure:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \acrfull{ce}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item[$\circ$] Provides overall technical leadership and ensures that all work aligns with the system concept and project objectives
        \item[$\circ$] Approves design choices and oversees integration of results from other roles
        \item[$\circ$] Acts as the main point of contact with the project owner and examiners
    \end{itemize}
    \item \acrfull{rm}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item[$\circ$] Responsible for eliciting, documenting, and maintaining requirements
        \item[$\circ$] Ensures traceability between stakeholder needs, system functions, and dependability objectives
        \item[$\circ$] Works closely with the \acrshort{vvm} to guarantee that all requirements are verifiable, and works with the \acrshort{sm} to ensure safety-critical requirements are properly captured.
    \end{itemize}
    \item \acrfull{sm}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item[$\circ$] Identifies hazards and defines safety goals
        \item[$\circ$] Ensures that safety considerations are incorporated into the design of the replanning protocol
        \item[$\circ$] Works closely with the \acrshort{ce} and \acrshort{rm} to embed safety into requirements and system architecture
    \end{itemize}
    \item \acrfull{qcm}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item[$\circ$] Maintains consistency and traceability across all project artefacts
        \item[$\circ$] Manages version control, document reviews, and configuration baselines
        \item[$\circ$] Supports all team members in adhering to quality processes and ensures that deliverables meet expected standards
    \end{itemize}
    \item \acrfull{vvm}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item[$\circ$] Defines the V\&V strategy, including test cases and fault injection scenarios
        \item[$\circ$] Ensures compliance with relevant standards
        \item[$\circ$] Works with the \acrshort{ce} to validate that the protocol meets stakeholder needs and with the \acrshort{rm} to ensure requirements are testable.
    \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}
