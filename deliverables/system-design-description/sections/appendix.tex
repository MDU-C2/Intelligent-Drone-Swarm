\subsection*{UAV Hardware}
A \acrshort{bdd} of the hardware inside a \acrshort{uav} was made to easier understand the design of the protocol. The \acrshort{bdd} is based on the design found in \cite{LuizNotPublished} with alterations to titles and the split of the one unit for Object Detection \& Collision Avoidance in to two units, as seen \cref{tab:uav-HW-names}.

%\vspace{0.2cm}

\subsubsection*{Hardware Components Overview}
The UAV architecture consists of the following major subsystems:

These subsystems interact with various sensors, actuators, and communication modules to execute flight, navigation, and safety-critical functions.
